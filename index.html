About TMCS

The Origin Of TMCS

In Tanzania, TMCS was established in 1984 at the University of Dar es Salaam (UDSM). Therefore, TMCS has now reached 40 years since its establishment. However, this union originated from the union of Catholic students in universities and middle-level colleges worldwide, known as IMCS (International Movement of Catholic Students). This union was founded in 1921 in the city of Fribourg, Switzerland.

IMCS was established with many goals, but the main objective was to support university and college students who could not afford to pay for their education. As a result, the IMCS motto became "preferential option for the poor."However, this motto was later revised from "preferential option for the poor" to "preferential option for the poor and mother earth."
